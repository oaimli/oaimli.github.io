Research Interests

Miao is interested in natural language generation (NLG), a subfield of artificial intelligence (AI), that aims to enable computers to generate natural language with deep understanding and effective reasoning. Throughout the years of pursuing the PhD, his primary area of interest revolves around investigating language generation conditioned on multiple documents mainly from the perspective of information integration and complex reasoning over the source documents, and making the generation process less opaque and more grounded.

In the long term, Miao’s overarching research goal is to (1) understand how humans correlate and integrate multi-source information with conscious reasoning for language production from first principles, (2) explore the potential of AI systems to achieve superhuman-level information integration and reasoning over multiple sources with voluminous and complex heterogeneous information and realize human-like communication conveying information based on devised consequences from them, and (3) develop high-quality evaluations on not only language generations but also inner workings of these intelligent systems for complex language generation tasks to make them useful and understandable.
