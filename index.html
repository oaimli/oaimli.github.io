Research Interests

Miao's research focused on enabling machines to understand, reason with, and generate natural language—advancing the fields of natural language processing (NLP) and artificial intelligence (AI). During his PhD, he has primarily worked on multi-document summarization, emphasizing information integration and decompositional reasoning with pre-trained language models to enhance transparency and grounding in summarization. His overarching research goal is to develop AI systems to enhance human productivity through human-AI collaboration, focusing on facilitating the synthesis of distributed information from multiple sources. His research interests include (1) gaining a deeper understanding of how humans cognitively correlate and integrate multi-source information in decision-making and problem-solving, (2) developing generative models that can reason over voluminous and heterogeneous information sources with intricate interdependencies at superhuman speeds, and (3) establishing high-quality evaluations not only for model outputs but also for their inner workings and behaviours in real-world applications.
